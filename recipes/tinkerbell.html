Tinkerbell

Description

Are fairies as tasty as fairy tails make them look? This project was supposed to be about a recipe, but I decided to make a meme!

Ingredients

  • Tinkerbell
  • A plate
  • Soup
  • Salsa valentina

Steps

  1. First, we take our plate and put the soup on it
  2. We proceed to ignore Tinkerbell until she loses her strength
  3. We take her (not from the wings!) and put her into the soup
  4. Take your salsa valentina and put as much as you like
  5. Enjoy! Follow my github for more recipes: fercodes
